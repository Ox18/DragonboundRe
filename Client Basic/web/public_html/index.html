Can't Login with Facebook?

1. Click "Reset Password"
2. Put your Facebook email address (the email that you had on your Facebook at the last time you were able to log in with Facebook to DragonBound).
3. Go to your email, click the link, and choose a new password.
4. Come back here and login with nickname(or email) and the new password.
No puedes ingresar por Facebook?

1. Click "Restablecer contraseña"
2. Coloca tu correo electronico de Facebook (El correo que tenias en tu Facebook la ultima vez que fuiste capaz de ingresar desde Facebook a DragonBound).
3. Ingresa a tu correo electronico, le das click al link y escoge una nueva contraseña.
4. Regresa aqua e ingresa con tu nickname (o correo electrinico) y la nueva contraseña.
